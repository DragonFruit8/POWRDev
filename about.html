ABOUT

Unveiling the Enigma: Discovering JT

JT is a problem solver that has learned that the underlying principles of good leadership stems from the emotional welfare of an individual, compassion for others, and the understanding that a team works best when they all feel valued. Coming from a variety of industry backgrounds from hospitality, to industrial manufacturing, he has grown to understand that his talents lye in connecting people with opportunities, collaborating a cohesive team environment, and making connections to abstract ideas. JT seeks to use his education in Simulation, Animation, and Gaming and work history to guide aspiring individuals to become better Business owners, brand ambassadors and to better convey their vision statement with the aid of Digital Graphic Design.

Why P.O.W.R. Design ?

P.O.W.R. Design is a newly formed business, meaning Phoenix Over Wavering Rapids, who aims to provide individuals with the tools and resources for personal growth. The journey began when JT was fed up with the nuance of capitalism and receiving value based on other's terms. P.O.W.R. Design tends to focus on achieving a fluid, flexible and flawless cooperation with an emphasis on sculpting small business' where it can evolve the best in the most efficient and streamline means. Reciprocity for all is a pillar that he are proud to divulge.
